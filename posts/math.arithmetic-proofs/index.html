Squared Numbers Equality Proof

Prove $\forall n,a\in\natnums$, $$ n^2 = (n+a)(n-a) + a^2 $$

Proof:

We can simply show the equalty holds true given some manipulation: $$ (n+a)(n-a) + a^2 \\ = n^2 -an +an -a^2 + a^2 \\ = n^2 \\ \square $$
